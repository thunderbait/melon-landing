Why Mela?

Mela is a technology solution that exists to reduce the time and cost currently being invested into event sourcing by large venues and maximise the time spent hosting events and bringing people together.

Plus more information about something or another.

Product

Mela is built to find and prioritise the best events and maximise your venues calendar space. We reduce the time spent manually searching and instead direct you straight to the people you need.

Lead Generation
Search for International & National events to fill in the dates in your venues calendar.
Lead Prioritisation
Organise events by the value they will bring and filter out all events that do not match your venue amenities.
Lead Nurturing
Track your lead progress and see insights into the event and surrounding area that can support a winning bid.
