The Mela 'Magic'

Lead Generation

  • Our Mela Magic begins with our database, multi-layered and ever-updating, it collects the details of past events, associations, venues and everything in-between.

  • Using machine-learning, our algorithm weaves the initial connections between prospective events and venues. The beauty of the Mela Magic is its network effect, crafting more intricate insights and thus ROI with increased usage of the platform.



Lead Prioritisation

  • Initial matches are then analysed, qualified and scored in terms of compatibility.

  • Financials to facilities, capacity to local economic impact, your calendar and much more inform our prioritization, are all taken into account. Adjustable depending on your own priorities, we qualify leads depending on what matters most to you.



Lead Cultivation

  • Learn about your new lead: their expectations, specifications and and what makes them tick.

  • Our Mela Magic gives you a head start in creating a meaningful and lasting partnership with your lead by breaking-down what ‘clicked’ between you.

  • These relationships can then be tracked and managed on our platform, priming you for future success.
